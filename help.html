FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

What is the process for adopting a pet?
First click on the "Animals" link, and select which animal species you want. Scroll through our adoption list that's frequently updated. If you find a little guy that you want, head over to the "Contact Us" page! We'll get back to you in 2-4 business days!

How much does it cost to adopt a pet?
The cost of adopting an animal depends on which animal you want to adopt. If you're interested in an animal, contact us and we'll give you a free quote!

What kind of animals are available to adopt?
We have a wide variety of dog breeds, cat breeds, and other animals such as birds, snakes and fish!

Are there any requirements for animal adoption?
You need to be over 18 years old, have proof of residency, and show that you have the ability to take care of a pet.

Tips and Tricks

Adopting a pet can be tricky if it's the first time doing it. Check out our newsletter for tips and tricks on how to be prepared!
